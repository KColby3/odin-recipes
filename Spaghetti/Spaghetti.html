Homemade Spaghetti

Description:

Sometimes you need a classic Italian night of red wine and pasta!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb Ground Beef
  • 1 medium Yellow Onion - chopped
  • 4 cloves Garlic - minced
  • 1 small Green Pepper - diced
  • 1 (28oz) can Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 (16oz)can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 (6oz) can Tomato Paste
  • 2 tspDried Oregano
  • 2 tspDried Basil
  • 1 tspSalt
  • 1/2 tspGround Black Pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine: Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stil until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetables tender - approximately 5-7minutes. Drain grease.
  2. Here Comes the Tomatoes!: Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occassionally.
  3. BON APPETIT! Serve hot and enjoy next to a nice glass of Red Wine!
